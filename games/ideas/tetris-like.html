Combo breaker - Tetris like

Basic Rules / Ideas

  • You have blocks of different colors/shape. When 3 of those blocks touch each other they breaks.
  • You have all the time you want to play every piece.
  • Every piece is a stick made of 3 blocks that you can rotate.
  • When you go forward with the game, you will get more and more advanced type of blocks until the point where it becomes very difficult.

Combos

  • When 5 or more block disapear in one break you get a length combo.
  • When other blocks break by the result of a previous break, you get a break combo.
  • Every combo gives you extra points.
  • If you reach 3 combos in one break your next block is a single explosive block that blows the surrounding blocks.
  • If you reach 5 combos in one break you next block is a single muticolor star block that match all the blocks.
  • If you reach 7 combos in one break the 3 bottom lines are cleared.

Music

Simple, light and soothing.

Visuals

Organic and pleasing to the eye. The block shape should be higly regonazible even for color blind people.

Explosion and Combo animation and the sound effects should be rewarding. The player should understand he did something awesome.
