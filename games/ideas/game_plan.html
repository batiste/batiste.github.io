Developing a game

I have developed a few simple prototype games during last years. Now I am serious into putting all the necessary efforts into producing a finished and polished game that is good enough to be sold on the App Store and the Android market.

For that I need to team up with people that can bring me motivation and the skills I miss.

I want to write the game using Javascript and Canvas. I know it's good enough for most of the games and especially for the games I want develop. The advantage of using HTML5 technologies is to have a cross plateform game without having to do much work.

To work on all devices the game's gameplay and input must remain simple.
