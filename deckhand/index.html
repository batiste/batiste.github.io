About DeckHand

DeckHand is a competitive card game that immerses you in a thrilling pirate adventure. Your goal is to outsmart your opponents by constructing the most formidable deck possible.

Easy to learn and quick to play DeckHand accommodates 2 to 4 players. It contains 72 cards, including 26 distinct card designs. Despite its apparent simplicity, the game offers a challenging mastery, with each game presenting unique dynamics based on the initial board state and player interactions.

Our design emphasis is on delivering an enjoyable, fast-paced game that provide a sense of crafting a distinctive deck with every play.
