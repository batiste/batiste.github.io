About Deckhand

Deckhand is an exciting card game that takes you on a swashbuckling adventure as a pirate. Your objective is to outwit your opponents and build the most formidable deck you can.

The game is simple to learn, fast, and can be played from 2 up to 4 players.

The deck of cards consist of only 72 cards, with 23 being complete unique designs. Despite its apparent simplicity the game is difficult to master and every game is different due to the initial board state.
