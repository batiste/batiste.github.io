Pirate Deck Builder Game

From 2 to 4 players. cards in the game.

  1. Each player receives a deck of 2 Sunset Plunder and 2 Celebrate: 4 cards in all. Remove all remaining Sunset Plunder and Celebrate from the game.
  2. All remaining cards are shuffled and form the supply deck. Reveal 6 cards by putting them face up in the center of the table, this is the supply. There should always be 6 supply cards face up.

Unfolding of the game, each player in turn:

  1. Apply effects that happens at the beginning of turn.
  2. Discard his/her hand and draws 3 cards.
  3. The player can do 2 actions of any kind during his/her turn:
    1. Playing a card: Apply its effects first, then put it in his/her discard pile.
    2. Buying a card from the supply: It goes in his/her discard pile.
  4. When your deck is empty immediately shuffle your discard pile: this is your new deck.

Game details:

  1. Treasures are small cubes that allow you to buy cards (cost of the card at the bottom right).
  2. +1 Card: Draw a card immediately.
  3. +1 Action: One more action of any kind during this turn.
  4. +1 Supply refresh: You may change one card from the supply. Put the old one at the bottom of the supply deck.
  5. +1 Treasure: Rake a treasure from the bank immediately.
  6. +1 Buy: You may buy a card without using an action this turn.
  7. The cards have 3 possible types: Attack, Structure, Adventure.
  8. Text that say "if you have an/a" checks the cards currently in your hand.
  9. Text on the cards has to be applied in order if it matters to the type checks.

The game ends when any player has 16 cards in total. End the round so that everyone has played. Add the number of hooks in each deck. The player with the most hooks wins the game. If there is equality, the player with the most treasure wins, then player with the most cards.
